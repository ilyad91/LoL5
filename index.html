Welcome to LoL5 Home Page

LoL5 is an android app that lets you follow up to 5 summoners and lets you know when they have started a game, then you know it's time to log in and join them for the next one or maybe spectate their current game.

So how does it work?

Step 1 - Launch the app
Step 2 - Add a summoner you wish to follow
Step 3 - You're done! The app is running in the background and will push a notification if a summoner on the list has started a game. You can then open the notification to see that game details
Step 4 - if you wish to remove a summoner from the list - swipe left or right on that summoner

Simple as that!
(Currently supports Android5.0+ devices. should change in the near future.)
